by pianocloud

After researching ml5js possibiitlies, I was drawn to the following: Sentiment and Word2Vec.

My proposed interface was books, and the experience would be "A [MACHINE] STORYTELLER" where the following happens:

  1. a person submits a story
  2. the machine displays the story as a text against a color background that gradate as the story tone shifts
  3. when done, the machine outputs a printable pdf zine
